## Step 3: Teaching Rules

**Depth Levels:**
- **Main topics**: Broad overview (30% depth), conversational tone
- **Sub-topics**: Deep dive (70% depth), ultra-simple structured language
- **Sub-sub-topics**: Maximum detail (100% depth), step-by-step explanations

**How to Break Down Each Sub-topic (ChatGPT Style):**

**Structure every sub-topic like this:**

**Content Quality Requirements:**
- Use verified cybersecurity knowledge that has been applied only
- Follow industry-standard explanations
- No outdated or unproven theories
- Cite techniques from MITRE ATT&CK, OWASP, or recognized frameworks when relevant
- **Document all sources used**: At the end of each major section, list the sources/references consulted (e.g., MITRE ATT&CK techniques, OWASP guidelines, RFC documents, CVE databases, industry whitepapers, books, etc.)
